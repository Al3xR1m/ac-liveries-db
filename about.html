About

AC Liveries DB — a community project for sim racers

What is AC Liveries DB?

AC Liveries DB is a free, community-driven database of liveries for Race Sim Studio mods on Assetto Corsa. The goal is simple: make it easy for every sim racer to find the livery they're looking for, in one place, without digging through forums and Discord servers.

You can browse by category, championship, or mod, upvote your favourites, and submit your own creations to share them with the community. All liveries are reviewed before publication to keep the database clean and reliable.

Made on free time

This project is built and maintained entirely for free, on personal time, out of passion for sim racing. There are no ads, no subscriptions, no paywalls. Everything here — the database, the search, the upvotes, the submission system — is available to every member of the community at no cost.

If you enjoy using this site, the best way to support it is to submit liveries, share it with other sim racers, and upvote the ones you love. That's all it takes to keep this going.

Submission guidelines
  • Only submit liveries for Race Sim Studio mods on Assetto Corsa.
  • Make sure the download link works and points to the actual livery file.
  • Add a screenshot — liveries without images get less visibility.
  • Mark your livery as Free or Paid honestly.
  • Don't submit liveries you didn't create without the original creator's permission.
  • All submissions go through moderation before appearing publicly.
